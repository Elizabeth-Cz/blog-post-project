    About Me

    Hi! I'm Liz and I LOVE to travel!

    Until I was about 20 I didn't travel so much, we went to see relatives in canada one in a while but those trips were all about family meals and some shopping(not that I'm complaining!)

    My first indepentent trip was to Italy, I was a 20 year old soldier and I saved up all of my money and vacation days to go on a two week trip to Italy with a friend and I was beyond excited. Italy was mind-blowing, we went to the most incredible museums, ate the best Italian food and had some great wine(well, great house wine, we were still on a pretty tight budget), and the people were the so friendly.

    Fast forwart to today, I can't stress how grateful I am that I had these opportunities to travel as much as I did, and I hope you'll like my point of view on traveling.

    Hope to see you here again soon!
